## Insights Supported by PowerBi Dashboards

- Global Sales Trends: Line charts show global sales peaking in the late 2000s, followed by a gradual decline.
- Genre Performance: Bar charts reveal Action and Sports as the top-performing genres by total global sales.
- Regional Contribution: Donut chart and regional breakdowns indicate North America as the largest contributor to global sales.
- Publisher Trends: Time-series and ranking visuals illustrate shifts in publisher dominance across console generations.
- Regional Genre trends - Stacked bar chart reveals that in North America and Europe action games are prominent but in Japanese gamers love more of Role-playing
